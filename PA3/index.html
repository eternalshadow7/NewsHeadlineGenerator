The Headline Of The Century!

My bot is a news bot that generates local news as a default, but when certain strings are typed, the bot will generate news for those topics. I decided to have it say "hello" in different languages because the news can be global. One of the actions I used was repeating the randomly selected "hello." This bot was based on what I see on my Facebook account every time I log in. I follow a bunch of magazines and news pages, and many of their headlines are so bizzare, especially the ones about celebrities, so I decided to make my bot make random headlines that are wacky most of the time. For the theme, I tried to make the page look like a newspaper, so using a bunch of shades of gray and putting the current date were intentional. I also used Times New Roman as the font intentionally to replicate the font typically seen on newspapers. I created a texture using a drawing program to make the background look like paper. I decided to add a header with text that moved because I wanted it to be like a news ticker.
